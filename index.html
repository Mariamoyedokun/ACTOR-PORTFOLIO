The Artistic Philosophy

Radical vulnerability.

For me, acting is the pursuit of depth and ambiguity in human behavior. I gravitate toward roles that challenge conventional heroism, seeking to reveal the complex, morally layered dimensions of every character. I believe that performance is not about wearing a mask, but stripping it away to find truth under imaginary circumstances.

I am deeply committed to building a body of work that is bold, socially aware, and globally resonant. Whether collaborating on contemporary screenplays or interpreting classical texts, my objective remains constant: to challenge perspectives and craft narratives that resonate far beyond the screen.
